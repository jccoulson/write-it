Exercise

Normal writing prompt, we set the environment and setting, just start there and go wherever the mind leads!

Example

Amidst the hustle and bustle of the royal kingdom of Magnora, a secret society operates unnoticed by regular townspeople. However, they play a significant role in shaping the kingdom's destiny. The moonlit meeting in the secluded castle's courtyard signals the unveiling of a secret so immense, it can reshape history. The society's members whisper amongst themselves under the cover of the ink-black night.

Challenge

Exercise

Writing prompt with a challenge, there will be some type of grammatical rule that you have to follow, good luck!

Example

In the heart of a bustling city, a mysterious character, known only as the "Caller," offers people the chance to change their fate. One day, you agonizingly decide to accept the unknown temptation, turning your mundane life on its head. The challenge is to write this in a second-person perspective but with the additional wrinkle that you can't use any contractions throughout the narrative.

Creative

Exercise

Creative prompt that starts with a fantastical setting, when writing for this don't be afraid to go off the rails!

Example

In a world where pastel clouds drift through the sky and the streets are filled with luminescent flowers, stands a city made entirely of watermelon sugar. The inhabitants are whimsical creatures, each unique, born from the sweet, pink sand that lays beneath the city. The city vibrates with the gentle hum of a thousand melodies only the city's dwellers can hear. One day, the melody changes.
